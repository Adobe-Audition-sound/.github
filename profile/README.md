# Adobe Audition — Professional Audio for Video Production and Sound Post-Production

## About Adobe Audition

Adobe Audition provides a complete professional audio workstation environment for video editors, sound designers, and post-production professionals. Adobe Audition combines multitrack mixing, spectral editing, and noise reduction. Adobe Audition supports Essential Sound panel, loudness metering, and restoration tools. Adobe Audition integrates seamlessly with Premiere Pro for round-trip video audio workflows. Adobe Audition enables users to clean dialogue, design sound effects, and master broadcast-ready audio. Adobe Audition is trusted by video editors, podcast producers, sound designers, and broadcast engineers worldwide.

## Deep Dive — Video Audio Post-Production Notes
Essential Sound panel with tagging for dialogue, music, SFX, and ambience
Multitrack mixing with clip grouping, automation, and submix routing
Spectral frequency display for visual noise removal and fault repair
Loudness normalization to ITU, EBU, and ATSC broadcast standards
Round-trip editing with Adobe Premiere Pro direct workflow integration

## Quick Reference for Adobe Audition

| Operation | Adobe Audition Action |
|-----------|----------------------|
| Essential Sound panel | Ctrl+Shift+E |
| Spectral frequency view | Shift+S |
| Noise reduction capture | Shift+P |
| DeNoise automatic | Ctrl+Shift+N |
| DeReverb processing | Ctrl+Shift+R |
| Loudness normalization | Ctrl+Shift+L |
| Match volume | Ctrl+Shift+V |
| Generate tone | Ctrl+T |
| Multitrack mixdown | Ctrl+Shift+M |
| Send to Premiere | Ctrl+Shift+E |
